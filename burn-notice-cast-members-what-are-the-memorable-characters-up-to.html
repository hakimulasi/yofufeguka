    Burn Notice Cast Members include Jeffrey Donovan, Gabrielle Anwar, Bruce Campbell, Sharon Gless, and Coby Bell. Most of these actors still make careers from acting to date, while others have slowly juggled other aspects of the industry such as writing, producing, and directing.

    The American espionage television series was created by Matt Nix and it originally aired on the USA Network for a total of seven seasons from June 28, 2007, to September 12, 2013. Burn Notice received generally positive reviews for the show’s pace, humor, dialogue, and combination of espionage and crime drama presented in an irreverent tone. Let’s meet the talents behind this great piece.

    Table of Contents

    Meet the Major Cast Members of Burn Notice

    1. Jeffrey Donovan

    • Date of Birth: May 11, 1968
    • Age: 55 years old
    • Role: Michael Westen

    Jeffrey Donovan is an American actor that is popularly recognized for his roles in Burn Notice. Jeffrey has continued to make a career in front of the camera and some of his notable works include Hitch (2005), Believe in Me (2006), Changeling (2008), and Come Early Morning (2006). In 2021, he was cast as a series regular detective for the 21st season of Law & Order.

    He has been married to Michelle Woods since 2012 and the pair state 3 kids together.

    2. Gabrielle Anwar

    • Date of Birth: February 04, 1970
    • Age: 54 years old
    • Role: Fiona Glenanne

    Gabrielle Anwar is an English and American actress; she is known for her television roles in series like Press Gang, The Tudors, and Burn Notice.

    She has been married twice and currently has 3 children namely Willow Anwar, Hugo Verea, and Paisley Verea. Her first union was with the actor John Verea and it lasted for 5 years (2000 – 2005). She later married Mark Malnik, son of Alvin Malnik in August 2015 in Montana. The couple presently lives in Palm Island, Florida.

    3. Bruce Campbell

    • Date of Birth: June 22, 1958
    • Age: 65 years old
    • Role: Sam Axe

    Born in Royal Oak, Michigan, U.S., Bruce Lorne Campbell made his professional entertainment debut in 1972. He is known for portraying Ash Williams in Sam Raimi’s Evil Dead franchise, beginning with the 1978 short film Within the Woods. In addition to acting and occasionally directing, Campbell has become a writer, starting with an autobiography, If Chins Could Kill: Confessions of a B Movie Actor, published in June 2001.

    Some of Campbell’s recent movies include Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022) and Evil Dead Rise (2023).

    Campbell’s first wife was Christine Deveau, whom he married in 1983. They had two children and divorced in 1989. The names of their kids are Andy Campbell and Rebecca Campbell. Bruce currently lives in Jacksonville, Oregon, with his second wife, costume designer Ida Gearon, whom he met on the set of the movie Mindwarp.

    4. Sharon Gless

    • Date of Birth: May 31, 1943
    • Age: 80 years old
    • Role: Madeline Westen

    Sharon Marguerite Gless is an American actress and author, who is known for her television roles as Maggie Philbin on Switch (1975–78). She has further featured in many critically acclaimed movies and series such as Cagney & Lacey (1982–88),  The Trials of Rosie O’Neill (1990–92), and Once Fallen (2010).

    The actress is known to have married the American tv producer Barney Rosenzweig in 1991.

    5. Coby Bell

    • Date of Birth: May 11, 1975
    • Age: 48 years old
    • Role: Jesse Porter

    Coby Bell was born in Orange County, California to a white mother and an African-American father in 1975. He attended San Jose State University, where he graduated with honors.

    Coby began his career with a few minor roles in various shows including The Parent ‘Hood, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, ER, and Smart Guy, before landing a thirteen-episode stint as Patrick Owen in L.A. Doctors. However, he is best known for playing Jesse Porter in the USA Network original series Burn Notice. Coby is also reckoned for his portrayal of Jason Pitts on The CW/BET comedy-drama The Game.

    On February 28, 2020, it was announced that Bell was cast as Captain Larry James, a Texas Ranger Captain, in the CW western crime drama series Walker reboot.

    Bell is currently married to Aviss Pinkney-Bell and has four children, three girls, and one boy.

    In addition to acting, Bell is also a musician and songwriter. He also serves as a mentor to underprivileged youths in the Big Brothers of America.
