    Career

    DJ Mustard started his career by collaborating with rapper YG. He has produced all the mixtapes of YG after his second mixtape called ‘The Real 4Fingaz’. In 2011, he produced the single “Rack City” by Tyga. It peaked at No. 1 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs and became a multi-platinum certified album by the RIAA.

    In 2012, he released the singles “Bitches Aint Shit” (YG), “T.O.” (Problem), and “We In Da Club” (Bow Wow). In the same year, he released 2 Chainz’s “I’m Different” and Young Jeezy’s “R.I.P..” They were certified platinum and gold respectively.

    In 2013, DJ Mustard released the platinum-certified singles “Headband” (B.o.B), “My Nigga” (YG), “Paranoid” (Ty Dolla Sign), “Show Me” (Kid Ink), “Up Down” (Do This All Way) (T-Pain), and “Na Na” (Trey Songz).

    DJ Mustard released also released many singles in 2014. Popular among them include Tinashe’s “2 On”, Ty Dolla Sign’s “Or Nah,” Kid Ink’s “Main Chick,” Jeremih’s “Don’t Tell ‘Em,” T.I.’s “No Mediocre,” Big Sean’s “I Don’t Fuck with You,” and Omarion’s “Post to Be.”

    He produced Nelly’s “The Fix” in 2015, which also became a popular single. In 2016, he released the hit single “Needed Me” by Rihanna. He has also released his own singles. In 2017, he released the singles “Want Her” and Run This.”

    As a singer, he has released the albums ’10 Summers’ and ‘’Cold Summer’ in 2014 and 2016 respectively. He has also released two mixtapes.

    Awards & Achievements

    At the 2014 BET Hip Hop Awards, DJ Mustard won the award of DJ of the Year. He also won the awards of Producer of the Year, Track of the Year, MVP of the Year, and was nominated for Best Club Banger. He has also won the DJ of the Year and Producer of the Year in BET Hip Hop Awards 2015. In 2015, he won 12 BMI R&B/Hip-Hop Awards.
