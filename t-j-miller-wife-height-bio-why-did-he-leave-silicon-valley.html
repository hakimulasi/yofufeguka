    His career has had many high points as well as a few low or better put – down controversies. One of the latter is his departure from Silicon Valley and here we will present a concise account of why he left the HBO comedy television series.

    T.J Miller’s Bio

    The general artist goes by his full name, Todd Joseph Miller. He was born on June 4, 1981 in Denver, Colorado as the son of Leslie Miller (Jewish mother) and Kent Miller (American father).

    As a young child, he was educated at Graland Country.Day School and East High School where he was known as the class clown and a member of the school drama club. After high school, TJ Miller went to George Washington University (GWU) in Washington, DC to study psychology with an emphasis on situational theory and social influence. Over time, he graduated with a BA in 2003. Prior to receiving his degree, Todd was a prominent member of RECS, a comedy group at GWU. He also studied Shakespeare for a summer. British American Drama Academy in London.

    Like any other actor who has a knack for making people laugh, TJ Miller began his entertainment career as a comedian when he moved to Chicago. He quickly rose to fame with an improv comedy company called The Second City which he toured with for 2 years. In 2011 he released his first comedy project called No Real Reason which was quickly followed by a comedy album titled Mash Up Audiofile in 2012.

    Along with Todd doing well with his comedy career, he was doing well on television as an actor with a few works like ABC Chelsea Lately (2007-8), Disney Channel’s cartoon Gravity Falls (2012) where he voiced Robbie Valentino, Fox TV series The Goodwin Games as Jimmy Goodwin (2013) and many more recent works like Silicon Valley (2014-2017) and F is for Family (2017).

    TJ has also been in a few movie projects like Cloverfield (2008) as Hudson “Hud” Platt, Hell and Back (2014) as Augie (voice role) and Ready Player One (2018) in i-R0k form.

    The comedian and actor has a rap comedy concept album to his credit released in 2011 titled The Extended Play EP His remix tape followed in 2012 – The Extended Play EP Illegal Art Remix Tape

    Who is his wife?

    T. J Miller is no longer single, this talented entertainer got his girlfriend and now wife – Kate Miller engaged in 2014. On September 6, 2015, the duo strolled down the lane at the Denver Botanical Gardens where they were pronounced husband and wife.. Their union produced no children.

    Kate just like her husband is also gifted in the arts, she is a mixed artist and actress who is doing well so far in her career.

    Why did he leave Silicon Valley?

    Todd Joseph Miller gained a lot of attention with his role as Erlich Bachman on the HBO sitcom Silicon Valley. In the show, he plays a prominent team at Pied Piper – a tech company where he’s a venture capitalist, software designer, and chronic pot smoker.

    Everything was going well for the actor, crew, and viewers until season 4 in 2017 when he decided to leave the show. As the media sought to better understand what was going on behind the scenes, TJ revealed, reported by The Hollywood Reporters, that he was offered a reduced number of episodes of the series and that he also hated Series director Alec Berg. Silicon Valley co-creator Mike Judge explained that it was clear that TJ was no longer on the show and so he decided to leave.

    Nonetheless, considering how his character was written out of the show, we could see the actor return to the show.
