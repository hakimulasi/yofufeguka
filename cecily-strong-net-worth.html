    Cecily Strong Full Overview and Wiki

    Early Life and Education

    Cecily Strong was born on February 8, 1984, in Springfield, Illinois, to parents Penelope Legler and William Bill Strong.

    Raised in Oak Park, Illinois, Strong’s early life was marked by a deep appreciation for comedy, particularly Saturday Night Live (SNL), which she often reenacted with friends.

    Her educational journey took her from Oak Park and River Forest High School to the prestigious California Institute of the Arts, where she honed her acting skills, graduating with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theatre.

    Rise to Fame

    Strong’s career trajectory took a significant turn when she joined the cast of SNL in 2012.

    Her debut as a featured player marked the beginning of what would become a decade-long association with the show, making her one of the longest-tenured female cast members in SNL history.
