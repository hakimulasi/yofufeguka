    Career

    Weathers interestingly participated in college football events and played on the defensive end in college. He started his career as a professional footballer under head coach Don Coryell from 1968 to 1969, for San Diego State Aztecs.

Eventually, raiders dropped weathers and were signed by the B.C Lions for the Canadian football league, where he played a total of 18 games till 1973. He got retired from football in 1974.

    After retirement, he decided to pursue his career in acting. His first-time screen appearance was seen in mark buster and Friday Foster. Weathers also starred in films like the predator, Action Jackson.
