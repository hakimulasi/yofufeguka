    Ginnifer Goodwin Talks &quot;Alien&quot; Pregnancy, Wedding to Josh Dallas

    Snow White, Prince Charming, and the circus! Ginnifer Goodwin opened up about her pregnancy and recent wedding to Josh Dallas on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on Wednesday, April 23, marking the first time she's ever publicly addressed either life milestone.

    19 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Snow White, Prince Charming, and the circus! Ginnifer Goodwin opened up about her pregnancy and recent wedding to Josh Dallas on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on Wednesday, April 23, marking the first time she's ever publicly addressed either life milestone.

    The Once Upon a Time star, 35, told Kimmel her child has been tumbling around in her tummy in a tumultuous fashion. "There's like, a Cirque du Soleil performance," she said of her bump. "It's like alien stuff." The actress joked about whether random people were "manhandling" her on the streets by rubbing her tummy. "I've been spared, I have been!" she exclaimed, though she added, "I feel a little less loved…"

    Kimmel then asked her about her recent wedding to Once costar Josh Dallas. As reported, the couple married in an intimate ceremony on April 12. "We wrapped production on Once Upon a Time, I think, two weeks ago," Goodwin recalled to Kimmel. "And a couple days later, I was wed in Venice, California."

    "I married Prince Charming," she added. "We're both Southern, so I was literally barefoot and pregnant at our wedding. I was afraid I would fall over, I don't know what I was thinking."

    "Were the parents mad you got pregnant first and then married?" Kimmel inquired. "No, no," Goodwin replied. "We may be Southern, but that's their first grandchild," she explained. "They're all coming back out here in a couple of weeks to camp out and wait," Goodwin said of her in-laws and parents anticipating the baby's birth.
