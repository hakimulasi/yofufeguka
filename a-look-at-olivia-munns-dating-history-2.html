    A Look At Olivia Munns Dating History

    Hollywood celebrity dating, depending on whose you decide to assess, usually has track records of multiple partners from the past. While actors and stakeholders in showbiz appear to switch between several relationships like the roles they play on screen, there are a considerable few who may have had a single misfire or two.

    American actress and model Olivia Munn is the case in point. Her love interests have not been widely diverse, but she sure has had her share of football and humor in her love life.

    Table of Contents

    • Aaron Rodgers (2014-2017)
    • John Mulaney (2021- )

    Aaron Rodgers (2014-2017)

    Munn was in a relationship with American NFL quarterback for Green Bay Packers, Aaron Rodgers, from 2014 to 2017. Munn revealed in an interview with Andy Cohen on SiriusXM’s Radio Andy that the split was influenced by a difficult rift between Rodgers and his family. According to Munn, Rodgers “hadn’t spoken to the parents and a brother for like eight months.”

    John Mulaney (2021- )

    Munn later dated American standup comedian John Mulaney. Mulaney, who had left rehab previously, praised Munn and their baby for helping save him from himself in the early season of recovery. The couple currently has a baby and remains lovers.
