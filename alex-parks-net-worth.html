    Early Life and Education

    Born Alexandra Rebecca Parks on July 26, 1984, in the serene village of Mount Hawke, Cornwall, Alex was the youngest of four siblings.

    Career Beginnings

    Alex Parks first came into public view when she won the second series of the BBC Television programme Fame Academy in 2003.
