    Early Life

    The American actor was born on January 20, 1957, in Santa Monica, California, to his actor parents. His father, Fernando Lamas, was an Argentine actor while his mother, Arlene Dahl, was a Norwegian American actress. Lorenzo was raised in Pacific Palisades, California. When he was a little kid, his parents got separated.
