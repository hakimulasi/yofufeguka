    Who is Mayilong0? Meet the Chinese Elon Musk.

    Elon Musk, the world’s richest man, appears to have found a Chinese lookalike.

    A man from Suzhou, whose real name is unknown, became an overnight phenomenon in the eastern Chinese province of Jiangsu due to his remarkable likeness to the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla.

    Their likeness has already caused so much controversy that the viral story has reached the 50-year-old billionaire himself.

    The similarities between the man and Musk were quickly noted by commentators, and the observation quickly circulated across Facebook and Twitter.

    According to News.com.au, Musk couldn’t resist the similarities, and joked, “Maybe I’m partially Chinese!” in a tweet made on December 20.

    Mayilong0’s TikTok Explored

    A Douyin user revealed himself as a Chinese lookalike of Elon Musk. In the TikTok video posted on December 3rd, 2021, he is standing in the driver’s side door of a sports car, speaking to the camera, smiling, and not moving much.

    That video received roughly 6,500 likes in four months.
