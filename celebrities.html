    Name:Kate Moss

    Birthday:January 16, 1974

    About

    Kate Moss, born January 16, 1974 in London, England, is a famous model known for her waifish figure and being the face of Calvin Klein in the mid-1990s. Her tumultuous lifestyle of partying and drugs has kept Moss in the media spotlight.

    In 2007, she launched her own collection featured in TopShop clothing stores. Moss has been famously linked to high profile men, including Johnny Depp and Mario Sorrenti. Her relationship with Pete Doherty, however, was notorious for being a root of many of her troubles with drugs. The couple finally split in July 2007.

    Shortly after her split with Doherty, Moss began dating Jamie Hince of the rock band The Kills. In February 2011, after a slew of false reports, the couple announced their engagement. They married five months later.
