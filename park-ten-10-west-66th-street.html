    Description of Park Ten at 10 West 66th Street

    Park Ten is a post-war cooperative located at 10 West 66th Street, just off Central Park. A full-time doorman and concierge are on staff, and amenities include a state-of-the-art fitness center, a children's playroom, a large multi-purpose/conference room, a circular driveway, a parking garage, and a landscaped roof deck.

    Park Ten is in a prime Upper West Side location near Lincoln Center, gourmet supermarkets, excellent restaurants, top shopping, and mulitple subway options. Pied-a-terres are permitted, and up to two dogs (30 pounds and under) are welcome.
