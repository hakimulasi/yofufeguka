    What is Glenn Scarpelli's Net Worth?

    Glenn Scarpelli is an American actor and singer who has a net worth of $1 million. Glenn Scarpelli was born in Staten Island, New York in July 1956. Glenn is the son of Archie Comics artist Henry Scarpelli. As an actor Glenn first starred in the 1975 movie Forced Entry. From 1980 to 1983 he starred as Alex Handris in the soap opera One Day at a Time. From 1983 to 1984 Scarpelli starred as Marc in the television series Jennifer Slept Here.

    He starred in the movies Nunzio, One-Trick Pony, They All Laughed, and The Favorite. Scarpelli also appeared in episodes of the TV series 3-2-1 Contact, The Love Boat, and MacGyver. He won a Young Artist Award in 1984 and was nominated for four others. In 1983 he released his self-titled debut album. Scarpelli came out as gay as an adult and established the Sedona Now Network with his former partner.
