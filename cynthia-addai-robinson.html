    The Lord of the Rings The Rings of Power Actress Cynthia Addai-Robinson Wiki | Bio

    Cynthia Addai-Robinson (born on January 12, 1985, Age: 37 years) might have started small but is now one of the most successful leading artists; she is a familiar face on TV screens and has full-length films to her name; some of her remarkable projects are “Star Trek Into Darkness,” “Texas Rising,” “The Accountant,” “Shooter,” and “Chicago Med.”

    Cynthia brings to screen an excellent presence and a proper portrayal of her characters; due to all her experience and hard work, she managed to bag the role of Queen Regent Míriel in Amazon Prime’s series “The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power,” let’s take a look at this talented actress’s film journey.


    When was Cynthia Addai-Robinson born?

    Cynthia Addai-Robinson was born on January 12, 1985, in London Capital of England, but her ethnicity is Ghanaian/American because her mother was from Ghana and farther from America.

    When she was 4, she moved to Washington DC, US, and was raised by her mother because her parents got separated, and under her mother’s supervision, she attended Montgomery Blair High School in Silver Spring, Maryland.

    At the same time, she grew interested in acting and wanted to try her hand at it, so after high school, she moved to New York and enrolled in the Tisch School of the Arts, from where she earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theater and after that began her studies at the Lee Strasberg Theater Institute.

    She took part in several Off-Broadway plays like “The Last Word,” “In Times of War,” “The Seagull,” “Bye Bye Birdie,” and “Four.”

    How Cynthia Addai-Robinson started her acting journey

    The 5 feet 6 inches tall actress Cynthia Addai made her debut in 2002 in an episode of “The Education of Max Bickford,” in the years to come, she appeared in several minor roles in shows like “Law & Order: Trial by Jury,” “Law & Order: Criminal Intent,” “Justice,” “CSI: Miami,” “Dirt,” “Entourage,” “Dash 4 Cash,” she landed a supporting role in a film “Mississippi Damned,” she landed a recurring role in “Flashforward,” and a role in 2 movies, “Edgar Floats,” and “Colombiana.”

    Her career took a jumpstart when she starred in a series “Spartacus,” for her role as Naevia, she trained in forced-style combat by Navy SEALs; she also got into “Star Trek Into Darkness” and had a minor part in “The Vampire Diaries.”

    The year 2015 was a busy year for her as she was seen in 2 significant roles for the series “Texas Rising” and “Arrow,” in the following years, she was offered characters in “The Accountant,” “Closure,” Cynthia also played leading roles in series like, “Shooter,” “Chicago Med,” “Power,” and “Always and Forever” it was a thriller film where she played Nicole Taylor, recently she signed a role in “The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power,” she has a major upcoming project as well, “The People We Hate at the Wedding.”
