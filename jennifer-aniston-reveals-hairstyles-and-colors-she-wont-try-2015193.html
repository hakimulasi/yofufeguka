    She's a creature of habit! Sorry guys, Jennifer Aniston won't be changing her signature honey-blonde long waves anytime soon. Even the Cake star's longtime hairstylist and BFF Chris McMillan can't get her to break out of her hair habits.

    01 Sep 2024, 00:00

    She's a creature of habit! 

    Sorry guys, Jennifer Aniston won't be changing her signature honey-blonde long waves anytime soon. Even the Cake star's longtime hairstylist and BFF Chris McMillan can't get her to break out of her hair habits. 

     

    "He wants to just cut it all off, kind of like a pixie, which I will never do," Aniston told Fashionista.com. "And he always likes to give it a good two inches every year, just a healthy cut." 

    Just as the star's keeping her length, she's not looking to pull a Kim Kardashian either. "I'm not a trend person really," Aniston said. "I kind of wait for the trends to go. Platinum blonde — unless it was a wig — no."

    After all, if it's not broke, don't fix it. Aniston, who co-owns the Living Proof hair care line, stuck to her signature red carpet mantra of simplicity for the Oscars 2015. "I just wanted to be comfortable that day," the 46-year-old said of her hairstyle, crafted by McMillan to complement her stunning Versace dress.

     

    "I don't like [to wear] my hair up very much. I'm not an undo kind of gal." She added, "I feel like if you're wearing a gown with glitter, you want to have beach, natural hair to kind of go against that. So that's sort of my main thing, being me."
