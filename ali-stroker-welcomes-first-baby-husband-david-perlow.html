    Its a boy! Ali Stroker secretly welcomes first child with husband David Perlow

    Source: PEOPLE.com Ali and David announced in July that they are expecting together with a sweet post on Instagram. The same day, the couple marked a milestone in their marriage a very happy first anniversary.

    29 Aug 2024, 00:00
    Updated On December 1, 2023 | Published By: Gracie Hale | In Child , Net Worth

    Source: PEOPLE.com

    • Ali Stroker is blessed with a baby boy with her husband David Perlow
    • The two kept a secret about the arrival of their baby for a month
    • They recently announced the birth of their first child a son named Jesse Kenneth Perlow, who is already one month old

    Ali and David announced in July that they are expecting together with a sweet post on Instagram. The same day, the couple marked a milestone in their marriage a very happy first anniversary.

    The happy news comes almost five months after their pregnancy announcement. However, the couple kept a secret about the arrival of their baby for a month.

    Ali Stroker and David Perlow welcomed their first baby together

    Ali Stroker and her husband David Perlow are officially mom and dad respectively. The beautiful couple, who announced their pregnancy news in July, welcomed their first child a baby boy.

    The Glee Project alum, 35, took to Instagram on Thursday, December 8, to announce the birth of their first child and also revealed the name of their baby boy i.e, Jesse Kenneth Perlow. Ali shared a gallery of pictures featuring the new baby born who is already one month old.

    The first photo of the series shows an adorable family picture of three alongside a signboard which holds by the new father David Perlow that reads “Jesse 1 Month Old.”

    In the next photo, Ali is seen cuddling with her newborn. Similarly, the other photos display the baby boy sharing some quality time with his parents.

    Also read Glee Star Ali Stroker is Expecting Her First Child with Husband David Perlow!

    Stroker then simply captioned the post,

    “World meet Jesse Kenneth Perlow! Born 11/8 healthy and so happy to be here! Life will never be the same. We are so lucky that you are our boy!”

    Ali and David first met in college and by fate, they reconnected during the 2015 Deaf West Theatre revival of Spring Awakening. After a while, they began dating and exchanged vows in July 2021.

    How is Ali Stroker taking on the new chapter of her life as a mother?

    While speaking to Byride, the Tony winner discussed her excitement about her motherhood journey back in August. She began,

    “As an actor, performer, and someone in the entertainment industry, taking on this new role as a mom and caregiver is exciting,”

    “I’m looking forward to being present and in the moment and learning so many new things. I can’t wait to share those discoveries with the world and other disabled parents.”

    She then continued,

    “I don’t think there is enough representation for disabled parents, and I want to help change that.”
