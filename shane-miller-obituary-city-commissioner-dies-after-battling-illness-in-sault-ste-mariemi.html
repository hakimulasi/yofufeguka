    Sault Ste. Marie, MI – The community of Sault Ste. Marie is grieving the loss of a cherished member, City Commissioner Shane Miller, who passed away on Monday, October 23, 2023, after a valiant battle with illness. The City of Sault Ste. Marie, MI, expressed their deep sorrow and heartfelt condolences to Shane’s family through an official announcement, mourning the loss of a remarkable individual who made significant contributions to the community he loved dearly.

    Shane Miller’s legacy as a dedicated City Commissioner began in 2017 when he was appointed to fill a commissioner vacancy. His commitment and passion for the well-being of Sault Ste. Marie led him to be elected to the City Commission in November 2019. Throughout his tenure, Commissioner Miller’s thoughtful participation in Commission meetings and his genuine friendship endeared him to colleagues and constituents alike. His impact on the community was immeasurable, shaping the city he served with care and dedication.

    Beyond his public service, Shane was known for his infectious laughter, clever humor, and the ability to light up any room with his presence. He brought smiles to the faces of friends and neighbors, especially during the holiday season when he joyfully portrayed Santa Claus for the children, seeking only the happiness and laughter he could bring to their lives. His generosity, kindness, and warm spirit endeared him to all who had the privilege of knowing him.In this time of grief, the community has come together to support Shane’s family during their difficult journey. Tiffany Schmidt, Shane’s beloved sister, has organized a fundraiser to help cover the expenses for Shane’s funeral. The community’s outpouring of love and generosity has already made a significant impact, reflecting the deep respect and affection people held for Commissioner Miller.

    To contribute to the fundraiser and honor Commissioner Shane Miller’s memory, you can donate on the dedicated GoFundMe page. Every contribution, no matter how small, is a testament to the profound impact Shane had on the lives of those around him.In honor of Commissioner Miller’s memory, the City of Sault Ste. Marie has lowered its flags to half-staff until the day after his funeral, symbolizing the community’s collective mourning for a beloved friend, colleague, and public servant. Shane’s legacy of kindness, compassion, and service will forever remain in the hearts of all who had the privilege of knowing him.
